## Content Trap Reminders (Exam Frequently Asked)

1. **Asana-Disease Mismatch Trap:**
   - ‚ùå Wrong: "Chakrasana is good for diabetes"
   - ‚úÖ Right: "Bhujangasana massages pancreas for diabetes"
   - This confusion appears in MCQs regularly

2. **Contraindication Oversight:**
   - Students often practice asanas that are contraindicated for their condition
   - Example: Person with high BP should avoid Chakrasana

3. **Procedure Order Trap:**
   - Breathing pattern is CRITICAL - wrong breathing nullifies benefit
   - Example: Bhujangasana requires specific inhalation/exhalation sequence

4. **Duration & Frequency Trap:**
   - Many think "more = better" but overholding causes injury
   - Different asanas have different safe hold times

5. **Sequence Logic Trap:**
   - Shavasana MUST be at end (not beginning or middle)
   - Warm-up essential before intense asanas
