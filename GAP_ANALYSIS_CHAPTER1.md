## Content Trap Reminders (From Verification)

**These are specifically mentioned as common exam pitfalls:**

1. **Bye Placement Inversion Trap:**
   - Teams (Odd N): Upper Half gets MORE
   - Byes (Odd Nb): Lower Half gets MORE
   - This inversion is INTENTIONAL and tested frequently

2. **League vs Knock-out Confusion:**
   - League = True winner determined (most accurate ranking)
   - Knock-out = Economical & fast (good teams might lose early)

3. **Bye Calculation:**
   - Must subtract N from NEXT HIGHER power of 2
   - Example: 13 teams ‚Üí next power is 16 ‚Üí Byes = 16 - 13 = 3

4. **Intramural Pipeline:**
   - Intramural = Mass participation foundation
   - Extramural = Elite selection (not all who participate)
